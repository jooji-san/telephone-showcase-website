Internet-Connected
Rotary Telephone

  • Familiar, Simple Design
  • Free Calls Worldwide
  • Remotely Configurable

Smartphones and the internet offer us many revolutionary services. However, as we combine more and more features into single devices, they become complex to use. Especially for the older generation. One of the most crucial features is internet-based calls. They are free and borderless. We want everyone to have access to this part of the internet. So we made a simple device just for that.
